Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015, our journey began in a small garage with just two people and a big dream. We were frustrated with the lack of high-quality, ethically sourced products in our industry and decided to take matters into our own hands.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business as word spread about our commitment to quality and sustainability. Today, we serve thousands of happy customers worldwide, but we've never lost sight of our original mission.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, ethically-made products that enhance our customers' lives while making a positive impact on our planet and communities.
